Traveling Notary Services

I am a fully bonded and insured (100k Errors and Omissions insurance) notary public in the state of Pennsylvania. As a mobile notary I am available to serve you 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. I travel to homes, hospitals, attorney's offices, businesses, nursing homes and beyond. I provide quick, efficient, and personalized Notary Services in and near the Philadelphia area.

Signing Agent Services

I am a licensed and background checked Signing Agent in the state of Pennsylvania, with certificates from both the National Notary Association as well as Notary2pro. I am fully bonded and insured with 100k Errors and Omissions insurance. I work with escrow companies, title companies and real estate agencies to provide a range of services from 1st and 2nd mortgages, new loans, home equity lines of credit, bilingual, reverse mortgages, loan modifications and more. As I am fully bilingual in English and Spanish, I am eager to service the Latino Community of Philadelphia.
